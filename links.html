Models Links

Scale models of cars are becoming ever more popular.
Here we suggest some online sources of models and information.


Just about everything is available, from Mille Miglia winning cars from the 1930's to production road cars of 2004.
We have selected mainly Italian companies since they have a natural tendency to produce more Italian cars.
Some of the sites are small, single manufacturer affairs, whilst some have online sales of numerous model makes. Some also offer second-hand collectors editions.
Suggestions for addition to this page are welcome.
