Fiat has been building cars with character for over 100 years.

This web site aims to provide information on all the cars developed and built by Fiat plus its prototypes. Information on the company, its motorsport activities and history are joined by a host of other features which should be of interest to the owner, mechanic, enthusiast or general reader.

There are 4 model specific pages which cover all production models since 1919, up to and including current production.
For each model there is an overview, technical details, illustrations, links, your comments and more.
